Greg Goelzhauser

I'm an associate professor in the Department of Political Science at Utah State University. I'm also co-founder and co-director of the Data and Politics Lab, which is a research incubator for an interdisciplinary team of faculty and students at Utah State University using data analytics to address important political questions. I study law and courts, emphasizing civil rights enforcement, marginalized group representation, strategic judicial avoidance, designing judicial institutions, and legal history.
